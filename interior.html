APPLE CARPLAY ULTRA ®

 

This next generation of CarPlay® brings together the best of Aston Martin and the best of iPhone for a truly integrated experience. Activated on ignition, CarPlay Ultra® offers a seamless experience across both the central screen and, for the first time ever, the driver instrument cluster. CarPlay Ultra® innovatively displays key vehicle information such as speed, RPM, fuel level and gear selection, as well as giving drivers control of functions such as heating, ventilation and active safety systems. This prestigious partnership is a world first, offering Aston Martin owners seamless integration, an abundance of new features and stunning levels of curated personalisation options*.
